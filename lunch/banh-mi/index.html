Banh Mi

Recipes!

Share

Original recipe yields 2 servings

Ingredients Directions
½ cup rice vinegar Step 1
¼ cup white sugar Combine rice vinegar, sugar, and water in a saucepan. Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring until sugar has dissolved, about 1 minute.
¼ cup water Allow the mixture to cool.
¼ cup matchstick-cut carrots Step 2
¼ cup peeled and matchstick-cut daikon radish Place carrot, radish, and onion in a bowl. Pour in cooled vinegar mixture and let marinate at least 30 minutes.
¼ cup thinly sliced white onion Step 3
1 skinless, boneless chicken breast half While vegetables marinate, set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the broiler. Lightly oil a slotted broiler pan.
1 pinch garlic salt, or to taste Step 4
ground black pepper to taste Sprinkle chicken breast with garlic salt and pepper.
1 (12 inch) French baguette Step 5
4 tablespoons mayonnaise Place chicken on the prepared pan and broil, turning once, until browned and no longer pink in the center, about 6 minutes per side.
¼ cup thinly sliced cucumber Step 6
1 tablespoon fresh cilantro leaves Place chicken on a cutting board. Cut into bite-sized pieces.
1 small jalapeno pepper, cut into 1/16-inch-thick matchsticks Step 7
1 wedge lime Slice baguette in half lengthwise. Pull out the soft center of the bread, leaving a cavity for the fillings.
Step 8
Lightly toast baguette under the broiler, 2 to 3 minutes.
Step 9
Drain off excess vinegar mixture after vegetables have marinated.
Step 10
To assemble the sandwich, spread each half of toasted baguette with mayonnaise. Layer chicken on the bottom half of the bread.
Top with cucumber, drained pickled vegetables, cilantro, and jalapeno. Squeeze lime wedge over fillings and cover with top half of baguette.
Step 11
Cut into two 6-inch sandwiches to serve.
