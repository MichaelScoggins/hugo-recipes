Chicken and Waffles

Recipes!

Share

Original recipe yields 4 servings

Ingredients Directions
4 eggs Step 1
¼ cup heavy cream Whisk together the eggs, cream, cayenne pepper, 1 tablespoon salt, and black pepper in a large bowl. In a paper bag, shake together the flour, cornstarch, and 1 tablespoon salt.
2 tablespoons cayenne pepper Step 2
1 tablespoon salt Dip the chicken into the beaten egg mixture, then place into the flour mixture and shake to coat. Place the breaded chicken onto a wire rack; do not stack.
1 tablespoon ground black pepper Let the chicken rest for 20 minutes to allow the coating to set.
2 cups all-purpose flour Step 3
1 cup cornstarch Heat about 3 inches of oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). In small batches, fry chicken 5 to 8 minutes until golden brown.
1 tablespoon salt Remove chicken, and drain on paper towels. Set aside or keep warm in a a low oven.
1 quart peanut oil for frying Step 4
8 chicken tenders Combine the mayonnaise, maple syrup, horseradish, and mustard powder in a medium bowl.
1 cup mayonnaise Place the bacon in a large, deep skillet, and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
¼ cup maple syrup Drain the bacon slices on a paper towel-lined plate.
2 teaspoons prepared horseradish Step 5
1 teaspoon dry mustard powder To assemble the sandwiches: Place 4 waffles on a cookie sheet, top each waffle with 2 chicken tenders, 3 slices of bacon, and 2 slices of Cheddar.
12 slices bacon Broil the sandwich for a 3 to 5 minutes until the cheese melts. Spread 3 tablespoons of the maple mayonnaise on the remaining 4 waffles and place on top of the sandwich.
8 thin slices Cheddar cheese
8 plain frozen waffles
